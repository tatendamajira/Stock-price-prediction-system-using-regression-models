How to Use Our Prediction Tools

Combine AI-powered sentiment and price forecasts for smarter, faster investment insights.

News Sentiment Analysis

This tool uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) to analyze thousands of recent articles, press releases, and blog posts related to a given stock.

It detects whether the overall tone of news is positive, negative, or neutral, and outputs a sentiment prediction with a confidence score.

Benefits:
  • Identify market hype or panic before price moves.
  • React early to major news events.
  • Complement technical or fundamental analysis with real-time emotional trends.
Best Used For:
  • Short-term trades.
  • Breaking news-driven price movements.

Regression Prediction Analysis

Our regression engine is trained on historical stock prices and technical indicators like moving averages, volume, RSI, and MACD. It forecasts future price movement trends.

The model uses statistical methods to estimate the likely price direction in the near future based on past data patterns.

Benefits:
  • Identify trend reversals or continuations.
  • Support entry/exit decisions with data-based projections.
  • Gauge medium-term investment opportunities.
Best Used For:
  • Swing trading or portfolio balancing.
  • Scenario testing based on price history.

Combining Both Approaches

Using sentiment and regression tools together allows traders and investors to get both emotional and statistical views of a stock:

  • Sentiment shows how the public and media feel right now.
  • Regression shows what the data suggests will happen next.

This gives a holistic perspective — helpful for both short-term and long-term strategy formulation.

Example Use Case

Stock: TSLA (Tesla Inc.)

  • Sentiment Analysis detects "Bullish" tone due to positive earnings and EV market expansion news.
  • Regression model predicts +4.5% movement in the next 7 days.
  • Interpretation: Combined indicators suggest a potential short-term upward price movement.

This kind of combined insight can be used to validate trade setups or flag new opportunities.

Tips for Better Results

  • Always verify predictions with current market events.
  • Don't rely on AI tools alone — use them to support human judgment.
  • Check multiple timeframes for consistency.
  • Use stop-losses to manage risk based on prediction confidence.
